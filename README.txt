# Freshmen Gameboard 4.0 (Ready to Deploy)

Place your /assets folder (with mission1.png ... mission20.png) next to these files when deploying.

1) In Google Apps Script, deploy your Web App (Anyone access) and copy the /exec URL.
2) Open config.js and replace the two URLs with your own (already prefilled with the one you provided).
3) Place your assets folder here with mission1.png ... mission20.png.
4) Upload to GitHub or drop the folder into Vercel to deploy.
